Research notes
Matching the table

The kitchen table is an IKEA extendable model. IKEA chairs are designed to work with IKEA tables dimensionally (seat height ~45–46 cm). SKOGSTA and LISABO pair especially well with a wooden extendable table.

Click-and-collect vs. delivery

IKEA OEZ (Hansastr. 68) and Eching are the closest Munich stores. Click-and-collect is same-day if the model is in stock. Home delivery for 4 chairs is typically free above €49 order value.

TOBIAS tip

Transparent chairs visually enlarge a small kitchen — a good choice if the room feels tight. The chrome legs wipe clean easily. Avoid if you prefer warmth over minimalism.
