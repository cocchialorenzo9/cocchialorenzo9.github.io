Research notes
Mattress compatibility

The mattress is already 140×200 cm. All frames listed are for this size. Confirm slats are included (IKEA frames typically need a separate LURÖY or LÖNSET slatted base unless noted).

IKEA vs Beliani delivery

IKEA delivers flat-pack; assembly required (~1 hour). Beliani delivers pre-assembled or with minimal assembly. IKEA stores in Munich (OEZ, Eching) for click-and-collect to skip delivery wait.

Storage beds

Ottoman beds (VION, LIEVIN) require ~30 cm clearance above the mattress to open. The gas-lift mechanism makes them easy for one person. Useful if wardrobe space is limited.

Price accuracy

Prices verified June 2026. Beliani runs frequent 30–40% promotions — check the site before buying. IKEA prices are fixed. All prices include German VAT.
