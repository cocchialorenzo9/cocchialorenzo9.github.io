Research notes
Mattress compatibility

The mattress is already 140×200 cm. All frames listed are for this size. Confirm slats are included (IKEA frames typically need a separate LURÖY or LÖNSET slatted base unless noted).

Delivery across retailers

IKEA delivers flat-pack; assembly required (~1 hour). Beliani and Otto deliver pre-assembled or with minimal assembly. IKEA stores in Munich (OEZ, Eching) offer click-and-collect to skip delivery wait.

Storage beds

Ottoman/lift beds (STILVORA, VION, LAMBRES, LIEVIN) need ~30 cm clearance above the mattress to open the gas-lift base. Drawer beds (BRIMNES, vidaXL, NORDLI) need clearance in front of the frame instead.

Price accuracy

Prices verified July 2026. Beliani and Otto run frequent 30–40% promotions — check each site before buying, especially Otto's discounted listings. IKEA prices are fixed. All prices include German VAT.
