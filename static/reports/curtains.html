Research notes
How many panels?

Standard: 2 panels per window. Each panel should be 1.5–2× the window width for good coverage when drawn closed. For a 120 cm window, two 140 cm panels work well.

Drop length

Measure from the curtain rod to the floor. Munich apartments typically have 250–270 cm ceilings. A 245–250 cm drop reaches the floor with a standard rod placement. 175 cm drops look short.

Eyelet vs. tunnel

Eyelets need a curtain rod (Gardinenstange) with a diameter ≤ 28 mm. Tunnel/tab-top need a rod or wire. Most apartments have a rail already — check before buying rods.

Munich stores

Roller has branches in Munich (Pasing, Moosach, Riem). IKEA at OEZ or Eching for click-and-collect. JYSK in Neuperlach and Dornach. All carry blackout curtains in-store.
