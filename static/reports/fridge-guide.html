Research notes
Price accuracy

All prices verified at mediamarkt.de on 25 June 2026. Prices include German VAT and may change. Promotions (e.g. Cashback) not factored in.

Choosing capacity

For 2 people: a 270–340 L combo is ideal. Minimum recommended: 60–80 L per person in the fridge section. For a built-in kitchen: measure the housing niche precisely (H × W × D) before ordering.

Energy cost impact

Energy class A (Siemens iQ300, ~112 kWh/yr) saves roughly €40–50/year vs class E (~280 kWh/yr). Over 10 years that's €400–500 — factors into tier comparison.

Munich delivery & purchase
