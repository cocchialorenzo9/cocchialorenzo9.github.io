Research notes
Price accuracy

All prices verified at mediamarkt.de in July 2026, incl. German VAT. Prices may change; "~" marks approximate values. Promotions and cashback offers not factored in.

Energy class C minimum

The EU 2021 re-labelling reset the scale. Budget models (mini-fridges, low-cost combos) now sit at D–G. Class C is the new practical baseline for a responsible purchase. Classes A, B, and C all qualify here.

Energy cost impact

At Munich's 2026 rate (~€0.33/kWh): class A at 108 kWh/yr costs ~€36/yr; class C at 171 kWh/yr costs ~€56/yr. That's ~€20/yr difference — €200 over 10 years — worth weighing against the price gap between tiers.

Choosing capacity

For 2 people: 300–380 L total is ideal. Allow ≥60 L per person in the fridge section. Measure your niche carefully: door clearance and ventilation gaps add ≥5 cm on each side.

No Frost

Eliminates manual defrosting and maintains more stable temperatures. All Tier 2–4 models with NoFrost have it in both compartments. The Koenic KFK 631-1 C IN (Tier 1) is the only model here without NoFrost.
