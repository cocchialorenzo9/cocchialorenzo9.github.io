Research notes
Price accuracy

All prices verified at mediamarkt.de on 28 June 2026. Prices include German VAT and may change. Cashback promotions not factored in.

Choosing capacity

For 2 people: 7–8 kg handles most loads comfortably. Step up to 9 kg if you wash bedding frequently. Larger drums are no less efficient when partially loaded thanks to load-sensing on Tier 2+ machines.

Noise (washing / spinning)

Noise is listed as washing dB / spinning dB. Spinning above 75 dB will be heard through walls in a typical Munich apartment. Tier 3+ machines stay below 70 dB — worth it if the machine is near the living area.

Munich delivery & installation

MediaMarkt and Saturn (Karlsplatz) offer Aufstellservice (~€39–59) to carry the machine in, connect hoses, and remove packaging. Strongly recommended for upper floors without a lift.

Water connection

German washing machines use a standard 3/4" cold-water inlet (G 3/4 angle valve). If the apartment has a single Waschmaschinenanschluss shared with a dishwasher, see the Dishwasher report for how to split the connection.

Energy cost impact

Class A (e.g. AEG L7FBF841N) uses ~155 kWh/yr vs ~220 kWh for class E at 220 cycles/yr. At Munich electricity rates (~€0.34/kWh) that's ~€22/yr savings — the AEG pays back its premium over Tier 1 in 5–6 years.
