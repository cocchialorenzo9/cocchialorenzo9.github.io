Research notes
What changed from the last draft

The closed-length cap tightened from 80cm to 60cm, and budget under €100 is now the priority. That drops the SIT Küchenwagen "Rustic" (75cm closed) and both Holzmoebelkontor solid-wood carts (85cm) from the previous draft — all three now exceed the new cap.

IKEA is the reliable budget source

ASKHOLMEN and NORBERG both have live, directly-confirmed ikea.de product pages. A NORBERG variant with built-in storage exists with a confirmed 15cm folded depth, but repeated fetches suggest it may not currently be orderable in Germany — stick with the plain NORBERG unless you confirm the storage version in-app.

Used market via Kleinanzeigen

Individual listing claims (specific prices, specific used units) didn't survive adversarial verification — the marketplace churns too fast to pin down a single listing as fact. Use the persistent search links, not a specific listing, and verify each one's actual dimensions and condition yourself.

Dimension confidence

"Confirmed" means an official spec page states the folded depth. "Estimated" means it was inferred from packaging dimensions or a sibling product's spec — plausible, but confirm before buying, especially for NORBERG, the SoBuy table, and JEOBEST.
