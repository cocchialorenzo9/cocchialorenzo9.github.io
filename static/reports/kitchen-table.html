Research notes
Why nothing fits perfectly

Iconic premium drop-leaf designs were considered and excluded: Carl Hansen's CH002 (Hans Wegner) closes to 90×90 cm — over the cap. &Tradition's HM6 drop-leaf (from €5,067) never publishes a folded-width figure despite checking the maker's own spec sheet and six retailers. High-end + ≤80cm-closed is a genuinely under-served niche.

Solid wood vs. the NNJN reference

The original NNJN table is laminate over MDF/particleboard. Research on material quality (medium confidence — sourced from furniture-industry blogs with a commercial angle, but corroborated on the core facts) supports solid wood as more moisture- and load-resistant for kitchen use, which is why every pick here upgrades to solid ash, birch, mango, or beech.

Bespoke sizing

Holzmoebelkontor's two carts ship at 85 cm standard — 5 cm over this kitchen's cap. They advertise made-to-order sizing; get written confirmation of an ≤80cm build and its price before committing, since neither claim survived automated verification.
