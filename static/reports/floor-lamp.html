Research notes
Product links

IKEA floor lamp URLs link to the Stehleuchten category page — the IKEA site restructures product URLs frequently. Use the model name as a search term on ikea.com/de to find the direct page.

Bulb types

E27 (large Edison screw) is the most common — use any standard LED bulb (warm white 2700K recommended for living rooms). E14 (small screw) needs a smaller LED. Both are widely available at dm, Rossmann, or any hardware store in Munich.

Arc lamps

Arc lamps require a heavy base to not tip over. Check the base weight before ordering — cheaper arc lamps can be unstable. Marble or concrete bases are best; thin plastic bases are a safety risk.

Where to browse in Munich

IKEA OEZ has a large lighting section — best to see lamps in person before buying. Lampenwelt.de is online-only but has the widest range. Depot and XXXLutz in Munich also stock floor lamps in the mid-range.
