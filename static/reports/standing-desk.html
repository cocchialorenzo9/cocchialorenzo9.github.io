Research notes
Price accuracy

IKEA prices verified directly on ikea.com/de (June 2026). FlexiSpot sale prices are promotion-dependent — check flexispot.de before purchasing. All prices include German VAT.

Not verified

Ergotopia, Uplift, and Linak were part of the research, but no specific price claim survived adversarial fact-checking. These brands may be available in Germany — independent verification recommended.

Delivery to Munich

IKEA and FlexiSpot ship nationwide including Munich. MeinOffice offers free shipping within Germany (excluding islands). Delivery times and assembly service should be requested separately.

Choosing dimensions

Ergonomically recommended: width ≥ 120 cm, depth ≥ 60 cm, height range at least 62–128 cm (for users 150–200 cm tall). For a dual-monitor setup: prefer 160 cm width.
